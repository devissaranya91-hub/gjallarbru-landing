PROBLEM SECTION

Healthcare workflows expose too much sensitive data.

When doctors and clinical staff use modern Artificial Intelligence to write medical notes, summarize patient cases, or analyze reports, that data often gets transmitted to external servers across the internet.

This creates significant privacy and compliance risks. Sending private patient files and medical histories to third-party AI platforms violates strict data privacy laws like the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and healthcare regulations. Clinics want to innovate, but they cannot risk serious privacy incidents and regulatory exposure.

SOLUTION SECTION

Secure AI workflows in real time.

GJALLARBRÚ sits directly between the clinic’s computer network and the AI model like an intelligent security checkpoint.

Before any medical text ever leaves the internal network, our software automatically intercepts it, scrubs away private identities, and secures the file in real time. The AI receives only the minimum clinical context required to perform its task, but it never sees who the patient actually is. We significantly reduce unnecessary data exposure, keeping your clinic fully compliant with PDPA standards before data ever crosses the perimeter.

WORKFLOW SECTION

Protect workflows without disrupting operations.

01. Check-In
02. Redaction
03. Tokenize
04. Access
05. Audit
06. Close

01. Check-In: Medical text or clinical requests enter the secure pipeline safely.
02. Redaction: The engine instantly detects and hides names, identification numbers, and personal health data to enforce PDPA compliance.
03. Tokenization: Private information is replaced with temporary, secure digital tokens.
04. Controlled Access: The cleaned data is sent to the AI safely, strictly managing data distribution.
05. Audit Trail: The system writes a permanent log showing exactly how the data was handled for continuous regulatory verification.
06. Session Close: The connection shuts down cleanly, completely deleting temporary processing files so nothing leaks.
