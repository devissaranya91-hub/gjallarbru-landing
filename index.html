Why the Name?

In Norse mythology, Gjallarbrú is the guarded bridge between protected realms. Gjallarbrú serves a similar purpose in healthcare AI — acting as a trust layer between sensitive clinical workflows and externalized model infrastructure.

01 / The Problem

Healthcare workflows expose too much sensitive data.

Data Exposure

Sensitive patient information may leave secure clinical environments during AI-assisted documentation or workflow operations.

Compliance Risks

Privacy, operational governance, and PDPA alignment liabilities become increasingly complex across institutional boundaries.

Operational Gap

Clinics require programmatic AI adoption pathways without increasing unnecessary underlying data exposure vectors.

02 / The Solution

Secure AI workflows in real time.

Trust Layer

Gjallarbrú sits directly between secure healthcare runtime systems and integrated AI environments.

Identity Protection

Detects, redacts, and separates core sensitive identifiers before external transmission payloads clear the perimeter.

Minimum Necessary Context

Downstream AI engines receive only the isolated clinical context required to execute the targeted task.
