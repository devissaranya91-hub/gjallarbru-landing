01 / The Problem

Healthcare workflows expose too much sensitive data.

Data Exposure

Clinical teams using AI tools may unintentionally send sensitive patient information outside secure clinic environments.

Compliance Risks

This creates privacy, governance, and PDPA compliance concerns across healthcare workflows.

Operational Gap

Clinics want AI adoption without increasing unnecessary patient data exposure.

02 / The Solution

Secure AI workflows in real time.

Trust Layer

Gjallarbrú sits directly between your clinical workflows and external AI systems.

Real-Time Protection

Sensitive identifiers are automatically detected, redacted, and separated before any AI processing begins.

Minimum Necessary Context

AI systems receive only the essential clinical context required for the task — completely isolated from core personal identities.
