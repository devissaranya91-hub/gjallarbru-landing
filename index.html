The Problem

Healthcare workflows expose too much sensitive data.

When clinical teams use AI to draft notes, summarize cases, or analyze reports, sensitive patient information may be sent outside the clinic environment.

This creates privacy, governance, and PDPA compliance concerns. Clinics want to adopt AI, but they need a safer way to protect patient identity before data reaches external tools.

The Solution

Secure AI workflows in real time.

Gjallarbrú acts as a trust layer between clinic workflows and AI systems.

Before data is sent to an AI model, Gjallarbrú detects sensitive fields, redacts patient identifiers, replaces identity with secure tokens, and records the session activity. The AI receives only the minimum clinical context needed — not unnecessary patient identity. This structure supports PDPA-aligned workflows.

The Workflow
01. Check-In

Clinical text or requests enter the secure pipeline.

02. Redaction

Names, IDs, contact details, and sensitive identifiers are masked.

03. Tokenization

Patient identity is replaced with temporary secure tokens.

04. Secure Routing

Cleaned clinical context is routed to approved AI systems.

05. Audit Trail

Each action is logged for review and accountability.

06. Session Close

Temporary session data is cleared based on policy.
